<Aside type="danger">
  We'll keep your data for 90 days after you delete your account. If you change
  your mind, you can restore your account within that time frame. After 90 days,
  we'll permanently delete your data. If you want to immediately delete your
  data, please [contact us](mailto:support@wattmaven.com).
</Aside>

## Steps

1. Go to the [console](https://console.wattmaven.com) and sign in.
2. Go to the [account deletion page](https://console.wattmaven.com/account/settings/danger).
3. Click the "Delete Account" button. 4. Confirm that you want to delete your account.

After you delete your account, you'll receive an email confirmation. If you don't receive the email, please check your
spam folder.

Any active teams that you own will be either deleted or transferred to another admin team member.
